Junior Winter Training 2025

HCC junior winter training for U11s, U13s and U15s

Each Thursday from 8 – 9pm starting 30th January through to 27th March at Honley High School.

Please use the for below to register and pay for your child. Rather than taking cash each week which takes up time at the start of each session this year we are charging a single flat fee of £35 to cover the cost of hiring the sports hall.

This also includes coaching from Mark Lawson, ECB Level 3 Coach and former Yorkshire, Middlesex, Derbyshire & Kent player.
