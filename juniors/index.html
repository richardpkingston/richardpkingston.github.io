2022 Junior Training

Friday 15th April (Good Friday): under 9s, 11s, 13s and 15s and continues each Friday from 6 – 7:30pm until the end of the summer.

Friday 22nd April: under 7s (years 1 and 2) starts with specialist coaches from Project Sports.

2022 Junior Fixtures

All junior fixtures of this season can be found here. Teams generally play at the following days and times:

  • U11s - Wednesday evenings meeting at 5:30 for a 6pm start. Please note that the U11 B team play their home games at Upperthong CC.
  • U13s - Sunday mornings meeting at 9am for a 9:30 start.
  • U15s - Monday evenings meeting at 5:30 for a 6pm start.
  • U17s - Wednesday evenings meeting at 5:30 for a 6pm start.
