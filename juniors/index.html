Honley Junior Cricket

Junior Training Age Groups

Junior training starts on Friday 18th April for under 9s, 11sa and 13s and continues each Friday until the end of the summer from 6pm - 7:30pm.

u7s start a week later in Friday 25th April. Reception and Year 1 from 5:45 - 6:30 followed by Year 2 from 6:45 - 7:30.

The u15s and u17s will train Tuesday's and/or Thursday's 6:30 - 8pm but you will be contacted by your coaches in the next week or so to confirm exact details.

  • U7s group 1 - Reception and Year 1 children Fridays 5.45 to 6.30pm.
  • U7s group 2 - Year 2 children Fridays 6.45 to 7.30pm.
  • U9s - years 3-4 (tournaments some Sat mornings).
  • U11s - years 5-6 (matches Wed evenings).
  • U13s - years 7-8 (matches Sun mornings).
  • U15s - years 9-10 (matches Mon evenings).
  • U17s - years 11-12 (matches Wed evenings).

Membership is required for all players and training sessions, and is available online here. If you want to try it out, please come along to a training session and speak to one of the coaches before you sign up. We are always looking for new players and welcome all abilities.

Junior Fixtures

All HCC fixtures of this season can be found here. Teams generally play at the following days and times:

  • U11s A & B - Wednesday evenings meeting at 5:30 for a 6pm start. Please note that the U11 B team play their home games at Almondbury CC.
  • U13s A & B - Sunday mornings meeting at 9am for a 9:30 start.
  • U15s - Monday evenings meeting at 5:30 for a 6pm start.
  • U17s - Wednesday evenings meeting at 5:30 for a 6pm start.
