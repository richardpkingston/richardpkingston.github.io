Safeguarding

Honley Cricket Club is fully committed to safeguarding and promoting the wellbeing of all its members. The club believes that it is important that members, coaches, administrators and parents associated with the club should, at all times, show respect and understanding for the safety and welfare of others. As part of our commitment to become an ECB Clubmark accredited, our goal is to provide opportunities for playing cricket, facilitate cricket development irrespective of age, gender, disability, race, religion, or sexual orientation and make the Club an inclusive, social environment that welcomes playing and non-playing members alike. As part of this we have established a set of values which will be explained at the start of the season.

Questions

Contact the club's safeguarding officer if you need clarification on any policy or reporting route.
