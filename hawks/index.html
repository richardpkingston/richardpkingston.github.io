About the Hawks

The Honley Hawks is the women’s and girls’ cricket pathway at Honley Cricket Club — built for confidence, skills, and enjoyment. Whether you’re completely new to cricket or returning to the game, there’s a place for you.

From 2026 we will be running:

  • U11s - Dynamos girls softball team
  • U15s - hardball girls team
  • Women's softball team

Training

Weekly sessions Women & Girls
  • Girls: Friday's — 6 - 7:30pm (ages u11s, u13s & u15s)
  • Women: Tuesday's — 7 - 8pm (all abilities)
  • Where: Honley Cricket Club, Far End Lane, Honley, HD9 6NS
  • What to bring: trainers, water, layers. We can help with kit.
